## Posting Rules — Twitter/X / 投稿ルール（Twitter/X）

1. **Post at least once per day.** Maintain minimum visibility in the feed.
2. **Use numbers.** 100+ enterprise clients, 40 books, 140 repos, $450M in projects. Concrete numbers beat abstract claims.
3. **Lead with results, not opinions.** Say "I built X" not "I think X." Credibility comes from proof of work.
4. **Welcome debate.** Reply thoughtfully to disagreements — engagement drives reach.
5. **Drive traffic to YouTube/Medium.** Include direct links max 3x per week (algorithm penalizes links). The rest should be indirect mentions.
6. **Post at 8-9 AM EST (US/EU overlap window).** This catches US morning and EU afternoon audiences simultaneously.
7. **Keep tweets under 240 characters when possible.** Short punchy posts outperform walls of text on Twitter.
8. **Quote-tweet your own threads on Day 2** to resurface them for followers in different timezones.

## Posting Rules — LinkedIn / 投稿ルール（LinkedIn）

1. **Post 2x per week** (Tuesday and Thursday recommended). Quality over quantity on LinkedIn.
2. **Longer form is fine.** LinkedIn rewards 800-1300 character posts with strong hooks.
3. **First line is everything.** LinkedIn truncates after ~2 lines. Open with a bold statement or surprising number.
4. **No external links in the post body.** LinkedIn suppresses posts with outbound links. Put links in the first comment instead.
5. **End with a question or call for discussion.** LinkedIn's algorithm boosts posts with comments.
6. **Use 3-5 hashtags maximum.** More than 5 looks spammy on LinkedIn.
7. **Tag relevant people or companies** when referencing real collaborations (sparingly — max 1-2 per post).
8. **Post between 8-10 AM EST** on weekdays for maximum B2B visibility.

## Hashtag Strategy / ハッシュタグ戦略

### Primary hashtags (use 2-3 per post, rotate):
- `#AI` — broad reach, high volume
- `#Productivity` — appeals to business audience
- `#NoCode` — growing community, high engagement
- `#OfficeAutomation` — niche but targeted B2B
- `#TrainingVideo` — for YouTube/tutorial content
- `#AITools` — discovery tag for tool-curious audience

### Secondary hashtags (use 1 per post when relevant):
- `#DigitalTransformation` — LinkedIn B2B audience
- `#CareerChange` — personal story posts
- `#KindlePublishing` — writing report posts
- `#ClaudeCode` — technical audience, product-specific
- `#SoloFounder` — indie builder community
- `#Over50` — age-as-advantage narrative posts

### Platform-specific rules:
- **Twitter/X:** 2-3 hashtags max per tweet. More looks spammy. Place at end of post.
- **LinkedIn:** 3-5 hashtags. Place at the very bottom, separated by a line break.
- **Never use hashtags in thread posts** (threads 2/ through 5/) — only in the first tweet of a thread.
