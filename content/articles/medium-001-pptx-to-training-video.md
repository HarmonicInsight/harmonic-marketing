## The Problem Nobody Talks About

Here's what most organizations deal with:

- **Training videos are expensive.** A 10-minute video costs $3,000–$10,000 when outsourced to an agency.
- **Updates are painful.** Every process change means re-shooting, re-editing, and re-paying.
- **PowerPoint assets go to waste.** Companies have hundreds of slide decks that could be training content — but nobody turns them into videos.
- **Cloud-based tools are a security risk.** If your training materials contain sensitive information, uploading them to a SaaS platform isn't an option.

Most "training video" solutions on the market ask you to either:

1. **Record your screen** (Loom) — but that's not a training video, it's a screen recording.
2. **Use AI avatars** (Synthesia) — but that feels artificial and requires uploading your content to the cloud.
3. **Build from scratch** (Vyond, Powtoon) — but that takes hours of animation work.

None of these solve the core problem: **you already have the content in PowerPoint. You just need it in video format.**
