Frequently Asked Questions

What PowerPoint formats are supported?

We support .pptx files created with PowerPoint 2016 and later. Animations, transitions, and embedded media are preserved during conversion.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Since it's a desktop application, core conversion features work fully offline. AI narration generation requires an internet connection.

How is my data kept secure?

All processing happens locally on your machine. Your files are never uploaded to any cloud server. This makes it safe for confidential, NDA-protected, and regulated content.

Can it integrate with our LMS?

The Business plan includes API access. We have proven integrations with major platforms including Moodle, SAP SuccessFactors, Cornerstone, and more.

What operating systems are supported?

Insight Training Studio runs on Windows 10/11 and macOS 12 (Monterey) or later. A Linux version is on our roadmap.

Is the one-time price really one-time?

Yes. You pay once and own the license. Major version upgrades are offered at a discounted upgrade price. Minor updates and patches are always free.
