Frequently asked questions

Does Insight AI Office really work 100% offline?

Yes. All AI models are bundled with the desktop application and run entirely on your local machine. No internet connection is needed for any feature — transcription, summarization, document editing, and batch processing all work offline. An optional internet connection is only needed for downloading software updates.

How does IAOF protect my data privacy?

Your data never leaves your device. There are no cloud servers, no accounts, and no telemetry. All processing is performed locally using on-device AI models. This makes IAOF suitable for environments with strict compliance requirements, including GDPR, HIPAA, and confidential legal or financial work.

What file formats are supported?

Insight AI Office supports a wide range of formats. For documents: DOCX, PDF, TXT, RTF, and XLSX. For meeting recordings: WAV, MP3, M4A, and direct microphone input. Export options include DOCX, PDF, TXT, and CSV for structured data extraction.

How do enterprise deployments work?

The Business plan includes enterprise deployment tools such as MSI/PKG installers for silent deployment, group policy support, and centralized license management. Your IT team can deploy IAOF across hundreds of machines using existing endpoint management tools like SCCM, Intune, or Jamf — no cloud infrastructure required.
